My Vegan Day

This blog is my personal collection of recipes that build my daily vegan diet. I always wanted to turn my day more green, healthy and interesting. It's a great place where I often visit to scroll through many ideas which influence my kitchen daily, make your belly happy and above all else - save my time and effort. I divided all the meals to three categories: breakfast, lunch and dinner. Like that I can have 3 regular satisfying dishes a day and I don't have to search the internet or look into my cook books. Everything I need is always here.
