Welcome to your Vegan Day

Our app is a collaboration for everyone who wants to turn his daily menu more green, healthy and interesting. For big meat lovers it's a great chance to try new, equally satisfying dishes, for vegetarians it's an eye-opener for even greener possibilities. It's a great place to scroll through many ideas which will influence your kitchen, make your belly happy and above all else - save you time and effort. Our goal is to connect people who are interested in vegan diet, build the collection of recipes and get inspired by others. Join and discover new flavors.
